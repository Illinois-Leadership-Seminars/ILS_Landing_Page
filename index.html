July Alumni Meeting, Elections, and Movie Night

  • July 29
  • 12:30 P.M. - 9 P.M.
  • 2512 N. Bosworth Ave #409, Chicago IL

  • Get excited! Our next alumni meeting will be a big one! We will be having elections for the 2017-2018 Alumni Board as well as discussing other things ILS. The meeting portion of the afternoon will be from 12:30-3:30 pm. Then, for those who wish to participate, we will be having a movie night from 4:00-9:00 pm. The movie night is completely optional, but it is always a great time to bond with some ILSers. Anyone under 21 attending the meeting must bring a signed one-page consent form. In addition, we ask that anyone staying for the movie night (even those over 21) bring $10 in order to cover the cost of food. Whatever money left over will count as a donation to ILS! If you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask anyone on the alumni board. We hope to see you there!

Corporate Board

The Corporate Board is rresponsible for the overall policy, financial stability, and direction of the organization, and delegate responsibility of day-to-day operations to the staff and committees. Board members must be age twenty-one (21) or older. The board receives no compensation. All board member positions are eligible for re-election annually.
